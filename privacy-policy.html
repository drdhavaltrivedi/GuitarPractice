Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Guitar Practice Companion ("we", "our", or "the App") handles user information and data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe and transparent experience using the App.

1. Personal Information Collection

We believe in absolute privacy. **Guitar Practice Companion does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information** of any kind. You are not required to create an account, register, or provide any personal details (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location) to use the App.

2. Data Storage

All data generated or entered by you within the App (such as practice schedules, metronome tempos, exercises completed, and mastery progress logs) is stored **locally and exclusively on your device** using local storage (AsyncStorage). We do not have access to this data, and it is never uploaded to any external or cloud servers.

3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

The App does not use any third-party analytics tools, ad networks, or tracking services. No data about your usage habits or device configuration is shared with third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect or transmit any personal data, it is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) standards. The App is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

5. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions to function correctly:

  • Wake Lock / Keep Awake: To prevent the screen from turning off while you are practicing or using the metronome.
  • Foreground Service: To allow metronome audio to continue playing when the app is in the background.

These permissions are only used locally to support the app's features and are never used to gather or transmit information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date". We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
